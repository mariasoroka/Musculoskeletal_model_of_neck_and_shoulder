This repository contains musculosceletal model of neck and shoulder and supplementary materials.
It is based on two works:
	1. Seth A, Dong M, Matias R and Delp S (2019) Muscle Contributions to Upper-Extremity Movement and Work From a Musculoskeletal Model of the Human Shoulder.
	Front. Neurorobot. 13:90. doi: 10.3389/fnbot.2019.00090 .
		Released under the CC BY 4.0 Use Agreement. (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/legalcode)
	2. Mortensen, J. D., Vasavada, A. N., & Merryweather, A. S. (2018). The inclusion of hyoid muscles improve moment generating capacity and dynamic simulations
	in musculoskeletal models of the head and neck. PloS one, 13(6), e0199912 . 
		Released under the MIT license.
